What is The FinTank 2015?

An event where awesome people collaborate intensively on Web & Mobile App projects. The FinTank will be over 48 hours of community collaboration and striving to win, by "Making an Impact" with City, County, or State Data. The FinTank's "Make an Impact" effort is made in support of the community. Cash Prizes will be awarded for innovation in the space of using public data to follow the theme and really change the way we live in South Florida.

Predite

Overall Winners

Predite is a mobile application that takes hold of every moment spent in the stadium during a competition, no matter what the challenge: Football, Baseball, Soccer, Tennis, etc...

Predite is a hybrid game/betting application that allows users to place small bets (for points) on every actionable outcome that a game has to offer. What will happen on the next snap? Will it be a fumble? What about the next pitch? Strike, Ball or Home Run? Who's the real king of the stadium? It could be you, just sign up and challenge your friends! Don't have any? Predite can help, itll match you with strangers who are also in the stadium! Social Micro Betting on Moments and Outcomes: Predite.
